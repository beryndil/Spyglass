# Themes

Spyglass includes 17 built-in color themes, plus Material You dynamic color support on Android 12+.

All themes use a gold accent color (#C9A84C dark / #806000 light) as the primary color, with Minecraft-inspired naming and color palettes.

## Dynamic Color (Material You)

On Android 12+ (API 31+), you can enable **Dynamic Color** in Settings > Appearance. This adapts the app's color scheme to match your device wallpaper and system theme. When enabled, the selected Spyglass theme is overridden by your system colors.

## High Contrast Mode

Available for all themes. Increases the contrast of text and UI elements for better readability. Modifies `onSurface`, `onSurfaceVariant`, and `onBackground` colors.

| Nether Red | #D32F2F | Nether-related content |
| Ender Purple | #AB47BC | End-related content |

| Emerald | #66BB6A | Trading and emerald content |

## Changing Themes

**Settings > Appearance > Theme** — tap any color swatch to preview and apply the theme immediately.
