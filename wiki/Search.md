# Search

Spyglass includes a global search that queries all game data categories simultaneously.

## How It Works

1. Tap the **Search** tab in the bottom navigation, or tap the search bar on the Home screen
2. Type at least 2 characters
3. Results appear in real-time, grouped by category

## Search Behavior

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Minimum query | 2 characters |
| Debounce | 300ms (waits for you to stop typing before searching) |
| Parallel search | All 11 categories searched concurrently |
| Ranking | Results sorted by relevance within each category |

## Categories Searched

Search queries across these 11 categories:

1. Blocks
2. Items
3. Recipes (by output item name)
4. Mobs
5. Biomes
6. Structures
7. Enchantments
8. Potions
9. Trades (by profession or item name)
10. Advancements
11. Commands

## Filters

- **Hide Unobtainable Blocks** — if enabled in Settings, unobtainable blocks are excluded from results
- **Edition filter** — results respect your selected Minecraft edition (Java/Bedrock)

## Navigation

Tap any search result to jump directly to its full detail page in the Browse tab. The app automatically switches to the correct browse category.
